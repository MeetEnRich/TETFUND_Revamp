Our Mandate & Core Values

The Tertiary Education Trust Fund (TETFund) was established to manage and disburse education tax to public tertiary institutions in Nigeria. Our core mission is to provide support, infrastructure, and training to ensure our institutions are equipped to foster world-class learning and research.

Key Areas of Focus:

  • Physical Infrastructure: Construction of state-of-the-art classrooms, lecture halls, laboratories, and offices.
  • Academic Staff Training: Postgraduate scholarships for lecturers in top domestic and foreign universities.
  • Research & Book Publication: Direct grants to support academic research journals and national reference books.
